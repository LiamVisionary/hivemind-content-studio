# Primary Faceless Shorts Plan

This is the first focused operating plan for MoneyPrinterTurbo as a faceless short-form content machine.

The goal is to build 1-3 faceless shorts channels that monetize through affiliate links first, with YouTube Shorts and TikTok Creator Rewards as upside.

## Strategy

Treat this like a content testing system, not a vibes channel.

The loop should be:

1. Pick a niche.
2. Produce a batch of shorts cheaply.
3. Publish consistently.
4. Track retention, follows, clicks, and revenue.
5. Kill weak formats.
6. Remix winners.

The first goal is not perfect videos. The first goal is to find a niche and format where repeatable production creates measurable attention and clicks.

## First Niche

Start with:

**AI tools for normal people**

Why this niche:

- Strong affiliate potential.
- Easy to produce faceless.
- Lots of search and recommendation demand.
- New tools create endless topics.
- The local AI stack gives us real credibility and examples.

Channel promise:

> Useful AI tools and workflows explained in under 30 seconds.

Target audience:

- founders
- creators
- students
- freelancers
- operators
- busy professionals who want practical AI use, not theory

## 14-Day Test

Produce 30 videos before judging the niche.

Posting plan:

- 2 videos/day on YouTube Shorts.
- 2 videos/day on TikTok.
- Reuse the same core video across both platforms.
- Slightly adapt TikTok hooks and captions.
- Publish manually at first.

Each video needs:

- Hook in the first 1-2 seconds.
- One useful idea.
- LocalTTS voiceover.
- Burned subtitles.
- Pexels/Pixabay footage, generated visuals, or screen recording.
- Clear CTA when there is a real affiliate/product angle.

Do not auto-post yet. Manual publishing keeps quality control high while the system is still being tuned.

## Monetization Setup

Affiliate revenue is the first serious money path because it can monetize before platform ad programs approve the accounts.

Before posting, create a simple link hub with:

- recommended AI tools
- newsletter or waitlist
- template pack placeholder
- tools/resources page

Affiliate priorities:

- AI SaaS with recurring commissions
- productivity tools
- creator tools
- AppSumo-style deals
- useful browser extensions

Avoid low-quality offers. The channel should build trust, not just farm clicks.

Track each link with UTM tags:

- platform
- video id
- niche
- format
- offer

## Metrics

Do not judge by views alone.

Track per video:

- views
- average view duration
- completion rate
- likes/views
- comments/views
- shares/views
- saves/views when available
- followers or subscribers gained
- link clicks
- affiliate clicks
- affiliate revenue

Winning early signals:

- high completion rate
- saves
- shares
- comments asking for the tool
- followers or subscribers per video
- link clicks from buyer-intent videos

Kill signals:

- after 30 videos, no followers, comments, saves, or clicks
- high views but zero clicks across buyer-intent videos
- videos are too generic to create repeat viewers
- audience response is curiosity-only with no conversion path

## Weekly Loop

Every 7 days:

1. Pick the top 5 videos by retention.
2. Pick the top 5 videos by clicks.
3. Identify the strongest format.
4. Make 3 variants of each winner.
5. Drop the worst 2 formats.
6. Add 10 new scripts.
7. Produce the next batch.

The first batch is reconnaissance. The second batch should already be sharper.
