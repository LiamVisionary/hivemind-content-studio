Create a 75-second animated brand explainer for [PRODUCT], aimed at [AUDIENCE], tone [TONE].

Structure:
- 0:00-0:12 — the problem as one specific scenario, not a list. A named person hitting the exact wall.
- 0:12-0:45 — how the product solves it, step by step, showing mechanics rather than claiming outcomes.
- 0:45-1:15 — the result in numbers: time saved, error rate, cost. Concrete figures only.
- Final line — one-line CTA.

Rules:
- No buzzwords. No generic enthusiasm. Just mechanics.
- Cohesive original visual identity across scenes: consistent character and environment anchors, cinematic animated feature look, high-contrast lighting.
- No logos, no real-people likenesses, no copyrighted characters, no on-screen text unless requested.
- Deliver scene image prompts, motion prompts, voiceover lines, music direction, and a publish title with tags.
