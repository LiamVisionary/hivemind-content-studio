Create a short UGC-style post for [PRODUCT] in the "what worked for me" note format.

Beats:
1. [Proof shot] a casual progress or result image/clip showing [RESULT] — phone-quality, imperfect framing, no studio polish.
2. [The note] cut to an iPhone Notes screenshot titled "what worked for me" listing 4-6 items: [LIST ITEMS]. The product is item 2 or 3, never item 1, dropped casually with no elaboration.
3. [Caption] undersell it: the energy of "my 3 month progress lol". No CTA.

Rules:
- Credibility is the mechanism; anything that smells like an ad breaks it. No brand voice, no feature claims, no links in the creative.
- The other list items must be genuinely useful and unbranded (sleep, protein, consistency) so the product mention reads as honest.
- Realism system applies to the proof shot: lived-in environment, phone-camera language, no beauty filter.
