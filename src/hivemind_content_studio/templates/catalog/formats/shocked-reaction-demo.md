Create a short UGC-style ad for [PRODUCT] in the shocked-reaction-plus-demo format. The product is the punchline, not the pitch — never open with the product.

Beats:
1. [0-2s | Reaction hook] a creator face with a deadpan or shocked reaction line, on-camera: "[REACTION LINE]" (the style of "paris girls are you OKAY???"). One to two seconds maximum. The line plants a question without naming the product.
2. [2s+ | Hard cut to demo] the product doing its ONE impressive thing: [CORE DEMO]. No explanation, no feature list — the demo answers the question the hook planted.
3. [Closing] casual first-person caption written like a friend texting, underselling on purpose.

Rules:
- The reaction must feel genuine, not performed; use the UGC realism system (locked character reference, named imperfections, phone-camera language, full negative stack).
- Exactly one product capability. Cutting a second feature doubles the format's strength.
- Measure shares and replies over likes; the post that flexes the product does numbers, the post that pitches it doesn't.
