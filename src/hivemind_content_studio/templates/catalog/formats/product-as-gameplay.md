Create a short vertical video where the core loop of [PRODUCT] plays as entertainment, in the product-as-gameplay format (the shape of notes turning into Subway Surfers-style gameplay).

Beats:
1. Open directly inside the loop already running: [CORE LOOP]. No intro, no talking head, no explanation.
2. Let the loop escalate visually — score climbing, streak building, content transforming — for the full runtime.
3. End mid-loop rather than with a pitch; the viewer should finish knowing exactly what the product does without being told.

Rules:
- This format only fits products with an inherently visual or absurd core loop. If the loop needs narration to be interesting, pick a different format.
- Show the loop raw: real screen capture or a faithful recreation, natural imperfect pacing, no marketing overlays.
- No selling words anywhere. Watch time is the mechanism; the product's usage loop is the message.
