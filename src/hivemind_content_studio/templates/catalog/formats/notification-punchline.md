Create a short UGC-style comedy ad for [PRODUCT] in the notification-as-punchline format.

Beats:
1. [Setup] an everyday scene where a notification would be maximally embarrassing: [WORST MOMENT] (a date, a meeting, meeting the parents).
2. [Punchline] the phone buzzes on screen and the notification reads: "[NOTIFICATION TEXT]" — written like an unhinged friend, screenshot-worthy on its own.
3. [Reaction] the creator's mortified or deadpan reaction, one beat, no explanation.

Rules:
- The punchline also demos the product: the viewer finishes knowing exactly what it does without being told.
- Write the product copy first and make it genuinely funny in isolation; the scenario only stages it.
- Keep the staged-embarrassment energy natural — realism system applies (locked character reference, phone-camera language, negative stack).
- The format is repeatable: same skeleton, new worst-moment and new notification each variation.
