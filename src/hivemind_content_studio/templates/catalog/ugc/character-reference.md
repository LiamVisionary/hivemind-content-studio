Produce one ultra-realistic vertical character reference photo for a UGC ad. This image becomes the locked character reference for every video scene, so realism here decides realism everywhere.

Ultra-realistic vertical iPhone photo of a [AGE]-year-old [GENDER], taken at a slightly awkward casual angle as if they just opened the camera app.

HAIR: [HAIR — e.g. shoulder-length wavy brown hair, slightly frizzy at the temples, a few flyaways at the crown].

SKIN & FACE: pick 5-6 specific named imperfections and keep them in every later scene — realistic visible pores across the nose and inner cheeks, faint redness around the nostrils, a small blemish near the jawline, slightly visible undereye darkness, one eyebrow sitting slightly higher than the other, lightly chapped lips with a small dry patch, scattered faint non-symmetric freckles. [ENERGY — e.g. relaxed neutral expression, mid-thought, not performing for the camera].

OUTFIT: casual lived-in clothing sitting normally on the body with subtle wrinkles and a slight fit imperfection.

The selfie is shot at chest height on the front-facing camera, phone hand visible at the bottom edge of frame, slight phone-camera motion blur on hair, mild lens distortion typical of front phone cameras.

PRODUCT (optional): holding [PRODUCT] naturally at chest height, label facing camera but held casually, every visible element of the product preserved exactly. If the product has any visible text, generate a separate clean product reference image first and attach both.

ENVIRONMENT: [SCENE — e.g. unmade bed with rumpled sheets behind, a charging cable trailing across the nightstand, a half-drunk glass of water, soft window daylight from the left so the right side of the face is slightly darker]. The room is not styled or curated.

Shot on iPhone, vertical 9:16, raw unedited HEIC-style photo aesthetic, natural lens distortion, slight chromatic aberration around high-contrast edges, mild grain in shadow areas, soft natural daylight only, no flash, no ring light, no studio lighting, no color grading, no skin smoothing, no beauty filter, no airbrushing, no AI-aesthetic styling, no posed model energy, no curated background, no commercial polish, no cinematic look. The photo should look like an accidentally-taken phone photo, not polished content-creator output.

Avoid AI-coded wording anywhere in the expanded prompts: no "stunning", "flawless", "ethereal", "glowy skin", "sharp cheekbones", or "natural beauty" — plain language wins ("brown hair" beats "lustrous chestnut waves").
